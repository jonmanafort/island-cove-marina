SEASONAL STORAGE AND CARE

We Have Solutions for All Seasons

WINTER STORAGE

Are you looking for a safe & secure place to keep your boat this winter? Look no further than Island Cove Marina. We offer a gated facility with on-site 24/7 Security. Our Travel Lifts can accommodate your boat (up to 35 Ton), or the state launch next door is a great way to drop off your boat for on trailer storage.

INDOOR HEATED STORAGE

Have you ever worried about the damage the elements are really doing to your boat in the winter months? We have just the solution for you. Introducing our Indoor Heated Storage facility. Affordable pricing, limited space, plenty of advantages. Call to Discuss Your Winter Storage Needs 860-388-0029
